Kendra Schwabel

Artist in Acrylics and Mixed Media


 
 

My acrylic paintings focus on capturing the quaint nostalgia of stately old buildings, street scenes and landscapes from old world Europe to my home in Minnesota.  Utilizing a rich, warm and  earthy palette, my paintings celebrate the everyday beauty found in familiar places and far away destinations.

I like to be adventuresome in my work by taking every painting a step beyond what is safe to something that is bold and exciting.

     MIXED MEDIA COLLAGE           


Collage Cards



Unique mixed media collage pieces focus on form, texture, line and color.  Each one is an original piece with it’s own characteristics and unique qualities.
 

My work is both representational and abstract, reflecting two distinctive approaches to the creative process.  In my paintings, I am representational, drawing from the world around me and focusing on detail and realism with my subjects.  In my mixed media pieces, I create from an abstract perspective, focusing on line, form and texture while drawing from within toward my final design.  Because of the diversity in my styles and the unique nature of each path, I remain inspired and challenged to take my art in new directions.
